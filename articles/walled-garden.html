walled garden

December 15th, 2025

A tree is probably the best thing out there. I talked to an acorn asking: "aren't you unsatisfied not being an oak tree?" To which they said "I am ontologically an acorn." Then they sang a pentatonic melody sounding in the upper register of a marimba because acorns are made of wood.
