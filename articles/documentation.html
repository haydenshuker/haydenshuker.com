With the goal of making this site as readable and copyable as possible, as well as to create a reference for myself, here is my own process for building this website.

Creation

  1. Get parts:
    • Get a domain name, I used godaddy for the sex appeal
    • Make those static page files, I used W3schools to learn how
    • Get a hosting service running. I used github pages because they are free, though there is a big technical skill barrier. I would otherwise recommend Neocities for my sort of goals.
  2. Link your host to your domain guys. This part ususally involves private companies. Most of the internet is surprisingly private, lets try to change that.
  3. Upload your site to your host.

Growing

My method is not to upgrade anything untill why I have breaks. Starting out, I kept only one page which I posted whole blogs until that became too big for finding anything.
